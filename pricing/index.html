Questions

Frequently Asked

What does "zero retention" mean?

We process your message, send the response, and keep nothing but a timestamp. Your email client is your archive. Nothing on our servers to hack, subpoena, or breach.

How do I pay?

Email admin@motd.com to set up your account. We'll send you a payment link and provision your token address once payment is confirmed.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Your access continues until the end of your billing period. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through.

Is there a refund policy?

If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days, we'll refund your payment in full. Try it risk-free.
